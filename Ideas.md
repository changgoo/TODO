# Project Ideas

## Turbulence Analysis for Idealized, Multiphase ISM

Study turbulence statistics in an idealized multiphase ISM setup with similar geometry and thermodynamics to TIGRESS, but without self-gravity. Turbulence is driven by supernova explosions, allowing controlled exploration of how the spatiotemporal correlations of SN driving affect multiphase structure and dynamics.

### Sub-experiments

1. **No temporal or spatial correlations** — SNe explode randomly in space and time; purely stochastic baseline reference
2. **Temporal correlation only** — SNe are temporally clustered (e.g., mimicking OB association lifetimes) but spatially random; introduces memory without spatial coherence
3. **Temporal and spatial correlations** — SNe are both temporally and spatially clustered, mimicking sequential explosions in star-forming regions; most physically realistic
